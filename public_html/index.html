About Us

Dolly Children Foundation (DFC) is an NGO that support the under - served child and position them for a better life

Mission:

To inspire the African child through educational aids and improve their socio-developmental skills through interventions centered on mental and intellectual programs.

What We Do

Reading clubs and after school programs.

Sponsorship Programs for Pupils from Under-Served Families.

Back to School Initiatives.

Training and Workshops for Teachers.

Construct and Refurbish School Facilities.
